Microneedling (CIT)

Collagen Induction Therapy (CIT), AKA microneedling, is a minimally invasive non-surgical procedure that involves the use of tiny micro-needles to reduce fine lines and deep wrinkles, hyper pigmentation, age spots, acne scars, stretch marks, and hair loss, and leads to improved volume, strength, resiliency, smoother texture, and a brighter complexion. This procedure helps your body produce the collagen it needs to create more youthful and healthier looking skin.

Dermaplaning

Dermaplaning is a non-invasive exfoliation procedure. It requires the use of a sterile surgical scalpel to gently “shave” the skin’s surface, removing the outer layer of dead skin along with Vellus hair (peach fuzz).
